Can Alabama Football End Up Back On Top in 2022?

2022-04-22

Alabama football has continuously been a team that shows up and shows out year after year. They continue to get players that know how to play the game and that dominate the field.

In the past ten years, the Alabama Crimson Tide have taken the College Football National Championship title four times. But this past year, Georgia beat them in a tough battle. Can Alabama manage to get back on top in 2022? Let’s take a look at the numbers and see what they need to focus on in order to pull another national championship title next year.

Turnovers can make a huge difference in the outcome of a winning or losing season for a football team. Let’s see where the top six teams that have been recent or previous national title winners stand in turnovers during the 2021 season.

Alabama, next to Ohio State, had the lowest amount of turnovers in 2021, losing out on 13 opportunities to score. With a higher number of turnovers, the Georgia Bulldogs still defeated Alabama in the national championship.

They had six first-round picks and ten total players drafted into the NFL after the 2021 season. A notable player that was a first-round pick and 15th overall pick is Mac Jones, who was a star player for Alabama for three years.

So the question still stands: Will Alabama be able to climb back to the top and take another national championship title in 2022? The numbers suggest so but they’ll need to keep their performance steady in order to do so.
