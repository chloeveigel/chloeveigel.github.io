Can Alabama Football End Up Back On Top in 2022?

2022-04-22

Alabama football has continuously been a team that shows up and shows out year after year. They continue to get players that know how to play the game and that dominate the field.

In the past ten years, the Alabama Crimson Tide have taken the College Football National Championship title four times. But this past year, Georgia beat them in a tough battle. Can Alabama manage to get back on top in 2022? Let’s take a look at the numbers and see what they need to focus on in order to pull another national championship title next year.

Passing completions can make or break a football team and their season. Let’s see where the top six teams that have been recent or previous national title winners stand in passing completions.

They had the highest number of passing completions in 2021, completing about 100 more passes during the season than the team who defeated them in this year’s national championship, the Georgia Bulldogs.

Let’s narrow in on the QBs for each of these teams and see how many passing attempts and completions they each have, and how many passing touchdowns they threw this season.

Would you look at that: Bryce Young, Alabama’s QB, is the front-runner in passing attempts, completions and passing touchdowns.

In seeing these two visuals, Alabama should be up on the charts in passing percentages, no? But wait…
